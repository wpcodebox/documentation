Installation
============

To install WPCodeBox, you will need to download the plugin from your account on the WPCodeBox website, then upload and activate it on your WordPress site.

### Step 1: Download WPCodeBox 2

1.  Log in to your account on the WPCodeBox website.
2.  Navigate to the "Downloads" section.
3.  Find WPCodeBox in the list of available downloads and click the "Download" button.
4.  Save the plugin ZIP file to your computer.

### Step 2: Upload WPCodeBox 2 to your WordPress Site

1.  Log in to your WordPress site's dashboard.
2.  Navigate to the "Plugins" section and click the "Add New" button.
3.  Click the "Upload Plugin" button at the top of the page.
4.  Click the "Choose File" button and select the WPCodeBox ZIP file you downloaded earlier.
5.  Click the "Install Now" button.
6.  Once the plugin has been installed, click the "Activate Plugin" button.

### Step 3: Verify that WPCodeBox 2 is Installed and Activated

1.  Navigate to the "Plugins" section of your WordPress site's dashboard.
2.  Find WPCodeBox in the list of installed plugins.
3.  Check that the plugin is listed as "Active".

Congratulations, you have successfully installed and activated WPCodeBox 2! You can now start managing your code snippets with advanced editing features.
